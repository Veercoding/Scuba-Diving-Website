The Baobab Beach Resort & Spa offers a wide variety of creative cuisines that are rich in flavor. With delectable exotic fruits, freshly caught seafood from the Indian Ocean, and a variety of delectable international and traditional meals served in stunning surroundings. Our buffets feature a new theme every night of the week to accommodate various tastes and preferences and to provide visitors access to a wider range of cuisines.

KARIBU RESTAURANT

With a large selection offered buffet-style and live cooking overlooking the ocean, the Karibu Restaurant offers breakfast, lunch, and supper.







JODARI RESTAURANT

A delicious array of foreign food is served at the all-inclusive restaurant Jodari, which is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner and has a view of the beach.
